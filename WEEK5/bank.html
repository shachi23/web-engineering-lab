BANK

Bank Jobs in India are now one of the most sought after career option among all the fresh graduates because Bank jobs are considered as the safe and finest jobs for fresher's and experienced candidates as well. Today every fresher are eager to write Bank exam so that they become eligible for recruitment in various Public Sector Banks.

To get a job in Banks, the candidate must have to crack the entrance exams conducted by the respective banks. The candidates who would crack the written test will be qualified for the interview and will be called for it. Based on their performance in both the tests, written and Interview, the candidates will be shortlisted.

Usually, Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) conducts a common entrance test on account of all public sector banks. The candidates have to qualify in the entrance exam conducted by IBPS to get govt bank jobs in India. To get Private Bank Jobs Candidates have to write written test held by Private sector banks.
